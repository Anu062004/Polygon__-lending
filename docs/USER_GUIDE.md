## Welcome to Debpol Protocol!

Debpol Protocol is a decentralized lending platform built on Polygon Amoy that allows you to:
- **Supply assets** and earn interest
- **Borrow against collateral** with competitive rates
- **Use flash loans** for arbitrage opportunities
- **Participate in governance** with DEBPOL tokens
- **Earn rewards** for platform participation

## ðŸ Quick Start

### 1. Connect Your Wallet
1. Click "Connect Wallet" in the top right
2. Choose your preferred wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.)
3. Switch to Polygon Amoy testnet
4. Get test MATIC from the [Polygon Faucet](https://faucet.polygon.technology/)

### 2. Get Test Tokens
1. Visit the "Faucet" section
2. Click "Get Test Tokens" for mUSDC and mBTC
3. Wait for transaction confirmation
4. Check your wallet balance

### 3. Start Lending
1. Go to "Supply" section
2. Select mUSDC or mBTC
3. Enter amount to supply
4. Click "Supply" and confirm transaction
5. Watch your balance grow with interest!

## âš¡ Flash Loans

### What are Flash Loans?
Flash loans are uncollateralized loans that must be repaid in the same transaction. Perfect for:
- Arbitrage opportunities
- Liquidation strategies
- Portfolio rebalancing
- DeFi strategies

### How to Use Flash Loans
1. **Navigate to Flash Loans**
   - Click on "Flash Loans" in the menu
   - Review available assets and fees

2. **Enter Parameters**
   - Select asset and amount
   - Set up your flash loan strategy

3. **Implement Strategy**
   - Write your flash loan logic
   - Ensure repayment within same transaction

4. **Execute Transaction**
   - Submit your flash loan transaction
   - Pay the flash loan fee (0.09%)
